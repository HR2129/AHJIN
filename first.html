Sung Jinwoo (성진우, Seong Jin-Wu) is the main protagonist of Solo Leveling. He is the strongest hunter in the world and the second Shadow Monarch, having inherited the position from his predecessor Ashborn. Jinwoo creates his own guild, the Ahjin Guild. Sung Jin Woo's Ahjin Guild is the strongest guild in Solo Leveling. After becoming an S-rank hunter, Jin-Woo, along with Yoo Jin-Ho, registered and created it to mainly help the former level up quickly.

"I'm going to protect my family, even if it means turning all the hunters in the world against me."

"Did I bother you by talking too much? People never really took notice of me."

Yoo Jinho is a D-rank hunter who becomes Sung Jinwoo's best friend after their first mission. Jinho is famous for going overboard with his equipment and buying expensive hefty weapons. Jinwoo surprises Jinho by saying that he wants to create a guild of his own, and Jinho will be the vice guild master of it, with Sung Jinwoo being the guild master.Later,both Sung Jinwoo and Jinho create a new guild named 'Ahjin Guild'. As three members are needed to create a guild, Jinho's cousin, Yoo-Soohyun, joins them.

Yoo Soohyun (유수현) is a famous Korean actress and model. She is also Jinho's cousin and an A-Rank Hunter under the Ahjin Guild. After learning from Jinwoo that he wanted someone who had no actual interest in hunting for the third member of their guild, Jinho gave Soohyun a call, as she fit the bill perfectly.Although she was initially reluctant to join out of the belief that Jinho was walking her into a scam, Soohyun immediately changed her tune after learning that he was working for the Sung Jinwoo and was delighted to join the Ahjin Guild as its third member, even eagerly asking Jinwoo if they could take a selfie together so that she could post it on her Instagram.

SHADOW ARMY 그림자

Shadows (그림자) are a race of undead beings that the Shadow Monarch(Sung Jinwoo_Guild-Master) brings into existence from defeated creatures and humans through the use of his Shadow Extraction ability. Each Shadow provides its own unique skills and Shadow's Authority benefit to aid in fight while clearing Gates and fighting high-level bosses. This makes certain Shadows much stronger than others and better suited in a team with particular hunters.While some shadow soldiers have abilities unique to themselves, they all share the same set of basic powers.

Abilities

  • Regeneration: Shadow soldiers possess incredible regenerative capabilities and cannot be killed by normal monsters, as they can completely regenerate even if their bodies are torn to shreds.
  • Growth: Shadow soldiers are able to steadily grow in power by killing monsters in battle.
  • Shadow Infiltration: At the Shadow Monarch's discretion, shadow soldiers are able to hide within the shadows of other humans. While doing so, they are able to watch and observe everything happening around the person that they are anchored to.
  • Transformation: A unique feature accessible to a master of the Authority over Death, shadow soldiers can be transformed into a weapon or any item to their master's whims.
